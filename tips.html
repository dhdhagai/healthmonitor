Health Tips

Here are some health tips:

  • Stay Hydrated: Drink at least 8 glasses of water a day to keep your body hydrated and functioning properly.
  • Eat a Balanced Diet: Include a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats in your diet.
  • Get Regular Exercise: Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise most days of the week to maintain a healthy weight and heart.
  • Keep up the goof work, make lifestyle adjustments to further reduce diabetes risk.
  • Get Enough Good Sleep: Poor sleep can drive insulin resistance, can disrupt your appetite hormones, and reduce your physical and mental performance.
  • Limit sugary drinks: Sugar-sweetened beverages increasing risk of heart disease and type 2 diabetes.
  • Don't fear coffee: Coffee is loaded with health benefits.
  • Eat nuts and seeds: They are incredibly nutritious.
  • Stay hrdrated: Hydration is an important and often overlooked marker of health. Staying hydrated helps ensure that your body is functioning optimally and that your blood volume is sufficient.
